Meet Zanele

Just a girl chasing big dreams with a heart full of purpose.

Hi, I'm Zanele Sbongile Radebe

I am 20 years old, born and raised in Katlehong, Johannesburg. Currently, I am pursuing my studies in Information Technology at the Tshwane University of Technology, fueled by a deep curiosity about how the digital world can be harnessed to create real-world change.

Beyond the screen and the classroom, I am a creative soul who finds joy in the art of baking and peace in the pages of a good book.

I am a lover of quiet mornings, deep conversations, and the loyal company of dogs. My life is guided by a simple belief: kindness and authenticity are the greatest gifts we can offer one another.My greatest purpose is found in The Unseen Generation—a movement I founded to empower youth who feel overlooked.

My dream is to see this movement prosper, becoming a powerful platform that represents young people with real stories and proves that your origin does not define your destination. I don’t have everything figured out yet, but I am committed to evolving every day. At my core, I’m just Zanele: a young woman learning, leading, and building a legacy of visibility for those who feel unheard. This is my story, and it’s only just beginning
